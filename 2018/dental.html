Dental Plans

McDonald’s offers two dental plans: Dental PPO Plus and Dental PPO Basic

Under both plans, you can use any dentist you choose. If you want to pay less out of pocket, look for a dentist in the BlueCare® Dental PPO network because the dentist’s fees have already been negotiated with these plans.

If you only expect to need preventive care, consider the Dental PPO Basic plan.It offers lower premiums, but has a lower annual benefit maximum (the most the plan will pay in a year); lower coinsurance (the percentage that the plan will pay for dental care); and does not cover orthodontia.

And good news! The dental plan premiums are not changing. They will remain the same as last year.

McDonald’s offers two dental plans: HMSA PPO and HMSA DHMO.

Both plans cover preventive care, such as annual check-ups and cleanings, but they differ in the cost of services. Learn about the plans to decide whether you want to take advantage of this benefit.
