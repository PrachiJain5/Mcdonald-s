It depends.

Consider:

  • Critical Illness Insurance
    It helps provide financial protection in the event of a covered serious illness. Some covered illnesses include heart attack, stroke and cancer.
  • Hospital Indemnity Insurance
    It helps provide financial protection in the event of a hospital stay, including planned visits for scheduled surgeries or having a baby and unexpected injuries or illnesses.
  • Accident Insurance
    It helps provide financial protection in the event of an accident, including a sports injury or car accident.
  • Supplemental Medical coverage
    It supports you when you or a family member is facing the significant out-of-pocket expenses that often result from a critical illness, accident, or hospital stay.
